Hackital is a hackathon in our nation’s capital. We assemble talented designers and developers from across the country, and work for 36-hours to create new technology. Join us in 2017, on November 11th and 12th.

Hackital is dedicated to developing technological solutions to online harassment through a partnership with Hack Harassment.

Want to get involved? Sign up above to receive updates. Send us mail or reach out @hackital.
We are an MLH member event and stand for inclusivity. See our Code of Conduct.
